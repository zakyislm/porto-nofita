# Site Settings & Security

The **Settings** page allows you to control the global identity of your website and manage security access.

## Site Identity & Links
- **Site Logo Title**: The text displayed on the top-left corner of the navigation bar.
- **Email Address**: The primary contact email shown in the footer and contact section.
- **Social Links**: Add URLs to your social media profiles (LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram, etc.). You can add as many as you need or remove existing ones.
- **Available For Work Badge**: Toggle the bouncing "Available For Work" sticker on your portrait. Turn it off if you are currently employed or not taking new freelance clients.

## SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
These fields help your website rank better on Google and look great when shared on social media.
- **Meta Title**: The title of the webpage shown in the browser tab and search results.
- **Meta Description**: A short summary of your portfolio.
- **Meta Keywords**: Words related to your profession (e.g., `portfolio, web developer, designer`).

## Authorized Users (Whitelist)
This section controls who can access the admin dashboard.
- **Add User**: Enter a Google email address and click "Add" to grant them access to the dashboard.
- **Remove User**: Click "Remove" next to an email address to revoke their access instantly.
- *Note: The primary admin email configured in the server environment variables (`.env`) cannot be removed from this interface to prevent you from accidentally locking yourself out.*
