# Managing Content

This guide explains how to update the different sections of your portfolio via the admin dashboard.

## 1. Hero & About
The Hero section is the first thing visitors see when they land on your website.
- **Title, Subtitle & Description**: These text fields appear prominently at the top of your site.
- **Portrait URL**: Provide a direct link to an image (e.g., `.jpg`, `.png`) or a video (`.mp4`, `.webm`) to display your portrait. 
- **CV URL**: Provide a link to your resume file (e.g., Google Drive link or PDF URL) so visitors can download it.
- **About Text**: Write a detailed bio about yourself.
- **Skills**: Add your technical skills or tools as comma-separated tags (e.g., `Figma, React, SEO`). These will appear as badges on the home page.

## 2. Projects
Showcase your best work in the Projects section.
- Click **Add Project** to create a new entry.
- Fill in the **Title** and **Description**.
- **Thumbnail URL**: Add a link to an image representing your project.
- **Links**: Optionally provide links to the live demo or the GitHub repository.
- To modify an existing project, click the **Edit** button. To delete one, click **Delete** (a confirmation window will appear to prevent accidental deletions).

## 3. Experience & Education
These sections work exactly like Projects.
- **Position / Degree**: Your job title or academic degree.
- **Organization / Institution**: Where you worked or studied.
- **Duration**: When this took place (e.g., "Jan 2023 - Present").
- **Description**: Detailed bullet points or a short paragraph describing your responsibilities or achievements.
