# Getting Started with the Admin Dashboard

Welcome to the content management guide for your portfolio! This documentation will help you understand how to manage and update your website's content without writing any code.

## Accessing the Dashboard

1. Scroll down to the very bottom of the home page (to the footer area).
2. Click the gear icon located next to the email link.
3. You will be redirected to the sign-in page if you are not already logged in.
4. Click the **"Sign in with Google"** button.

## Authorization

Your portfolio is secure. Only email addresses that have been explicitly whitelisted can access the admin dashboard. 
- If someone tries to log in with an unauthorized Google account, they will be redirected to the home page with an "Access Denied" error message.
- You can manage who has access to the dashboard from the **Settings** menu.

## Navigating the Admin Area

Once logged in, you will see a navigation sidebar on the left containing the following sections:
- **Dashboard**: A quick overview of your content and recent activity.
- **Hero & About**: Update your main introduction, profile picture, CV, and bio.
- **Projects**: Add, edit, or remove your featured works.
- **Experience**: Manage your professional work history.
- **Education**: Manage your academic background.
- **Settings**: Update global site settings, social links, SEO, and authorized users.
- **Sign Out**: Securely log out of the admin panel.
